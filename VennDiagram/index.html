Intersections in Film Selections

Comparing Mainstream Awards and Selections

A Venn Diagram which compares different film lists/awards to demonstrate intersections between the selections. Javascript calculates layouts that demonstrate significant intersections, but not every intersection is possible to show.

Lists used for this diagram: Academy Award nominees, International Submissions to the Academy Awards, the AFI Top 100 in 1998 and 2008, Roger Ebert's "Great Movie" selections, and the New York Times' Essential 1,000 Films to See.

And awards from the "big four" film critic associations: Los Angeles Film Critic Association, National Board of Review, National Society of Film Critics, and New York Film Critic Circle.
